Hyperparameters to replicate results from Section 7.

1. DLasso:
    - Basis (X and Z): Cubic polynomials with all interactions
    - for all k and n_obs:
        - dlasso_feat_degree = 3
        - dlasso_cv = 3
        - dlasso_fs_alpha = 0.0001
        - dlasso_tol = 0.001
        - dlasso_alphas = np.logspace(-7, -1, 100)

2. DML:
    - Basis (X and Z): cubic polynomials with only pairwise interactions
    - for all k and n_obs:
        - dml_n_folds = 5
        - dml_feat_degree = 3

3. ADMLIV:
    - Basis (X and Z): Cubic polynomials with all interactions
    - admliv_n_folds = 5
    - admliv_use_adaptive_pgmm = True
    - admliv_use_cv_for_pgmm = False
    - for k = 2 and all n_obs:
        - pgmm_c = 0.01
    - for k = 5 and all n_obs:
        - pgmm_c = 0.001
    - for k = 10 and all n_obs:
        - pgmm_c = 0.0001
